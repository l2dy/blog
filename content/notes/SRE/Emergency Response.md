## Playbooks

Humans add latency, so thinking through and recording the best practices ahead of time in a "playbook".
It's also a substitute for smart engineers able to think on the fly, and helps response to a high-stakes or time-sensitive page.

## Runbooks

A runbook is a set of instructions for completing a routine task.

### Runbook Discoverability

A runbook template should include a section at the top describing the intent of the runbook in one sentence.
