To copy text from iTerm2 with keyboard only, press `s-S-c` to activate copy mode.

In copy mode, press `v`, `V` or `C-v` to toggle selection by character, line, or rectangle, similar to Vim's Visual mode. `hjkl` and word-based movement keys are also supported.

To copy the selection, press `y`. To quit copy mode, press `q`.
