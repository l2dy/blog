## Regions

Each Region provides AWS services independently.

## Availability Zones

AZs are less than 2ms apart and highly connected with redundant paths inter-AZ and to transit centers.

## Transit Center

Transit centers handle connections to other AWS Regions, Direct Connect private links, and Internet through peering and paid transit.
